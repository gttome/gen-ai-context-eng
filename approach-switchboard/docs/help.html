What this app does

Approach Switchboard helps you decide whether your task needs basic prompting, context engineering, retrieval (RAG), or fine-tuning—based on Chapter 1 concepts.

How to use it

  1. Pick a scenario type.
  2. Toggle the signals that match your real situation.
  3. Read the recommendation and follow the “What to do next” checklist.
  4. Copy or download the recommendation and paste it into your workflow.

Quick start (recommended)

  1. Stay in Simple mode if you’re new (it hides advanced tools).
  2. Tap a Demo story to see a realistic example instantly.
  3. Tap Why? on a signal to learn what it changes.
  4. Use the Tour button anytime for a guided walkthrough.

Snapshots

Use Snapshots to save up to 10 decision states locally, then export/import history as JSON for comparison across scenarios.
