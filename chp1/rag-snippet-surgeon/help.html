This is a lightweight placeholder page. It explains how to use the MVP.

  1. Choose a question (top section).
  2. Select 2–6 excerpts. Keep them short and relevant.
  3. Tap Simulate grounded answer to see whether your evidence is sufficient.
  4. Copy the Grounding Knowledge block into your context skeleton.
  5. If the result says evidence is missing, swap excerpts and retry.

Tip: enable Scalpel Mode to trim excerpts down to the 1–2 sentences that actually matter.
